Who I Am

ABOUT ME

I'm Jai Karthick, a passionate Video Editor based in Chennai with a strong eye for detail and a deep love for visual storytelling. Every frame I cut is driven by emotion, rhythm, and purpose.

From fast-paced social media reels to cinematic political campaigns and brand commercials — I bring creative vision and technical precision to every project I work on.

I work fluently across industry-standard tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve, and cutting-edge AI tools like Google Veo3 and Nano Banana to push creative boundaries.

During my internships at Demos Project Pvt. Ltd. and Noobie Kid Agency, I honed my skills in deadline-driven environments, collaborated with creative teams, and delivered content that performed.

I believe great editing is invisible — the audience should feel the story, not see the cut. That philosophy guides everything I create, from the first import to the final export.

Currently open to freelance projects, full-time roles, and creative collaborations. If you have a story to tell, I have the skills to make it unforgettable.

Let's build something extraordinary together — one frame at a time. 🎬
